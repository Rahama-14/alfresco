Training

  


During the initial release period Alfresco will provide guided training on a case by case basis. Please contact to discuss your training requirements.

Alfresco Training Services provides information technology professionals with substantive, targeted educational experiences. Our online, instructor-led courses are specifically designed to fit tight budgets and busy schedules. Our instructors are domain experts with extensive backgrounds in training and mentoring.

Our teaching methods and standards combine practical, informative lectures with relevant, hands-on lab sessions. Labs begin with basic subject matter concepts, and progressively challenge students to apply new concepts and knowledge learned through class lectures.

Students receive a full complement of soft copy materials for each course, including Lecture Workbooks, Lab Exercises, and any supporting software files required to complete the lab exercises.
