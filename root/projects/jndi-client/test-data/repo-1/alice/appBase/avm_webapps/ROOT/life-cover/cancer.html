GIVING THE BIG 'C' THE BIG 'V'

Since you’re looking into life insurance, we know you’re already thinking about those ‘what ifs’ in life that we’d all rather push to the back of our minds.

But have you ever thought what might happen if you were to get cancer? Chances are, 1 in 6 of us will get it before we're 70*. Whether you’re a fitness fanatic or a couch potato, cancer doesn’t seem to care.

With Virgin Cancer Cover you’d get a pot of cash to help you fight back, get alternative treatment, keep paying the bills, take a holiday, whatever – leaving you to focus on important things, like getting better.

WHAT IS VIRGIN CANCER COVER?

Virgin Cancer Cover is custom designed to help you cope with the financial impact of the big ‘C’. There’s no other insurance product in the UK that offers our unique range of benefits combined with such comprehensive cancer cover at such an affordable price.

It’s pretty special because it’s designed to let you claim at earlier stages of cancer than other types of policy. And you can claim more than once if you need to, up to your maximum cover amount. 

Plus it includes cover for death and terminal illness, like you get from our normal life cover.

cancer too.
