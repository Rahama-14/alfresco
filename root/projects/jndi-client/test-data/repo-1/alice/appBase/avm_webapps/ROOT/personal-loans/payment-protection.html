What if?

Nobody likes to think about getting ill or losing their job. Or worse. We all tend to think, 'nah, it won't happen to me!'. But there’s always that nagging question…what if?

That’s why Payment Protection Cover is something you should seriously think about.

  • It covers your Virgin Personal Loan repayments in full if you have an accident, become ill, or suddenly find yourself out of a job through no fault of your own.
  • We backdate your money to the first day your claim started.
  • It’s got valuable life cover included – so if you’re suddenly not around to worry about your loan repayments, nobody else will have to either.
  • We take care of your payments from the first day you make a claim.            

Sleep easier. Sort it out now and forget about it.

For a more in-depth overview of what you get with Virgin Personal Loan Payment Protection Cover click here. It might not be riveting, but it is important, so please read it carefully.

Payment Protection Cover is arranged for Virign Personal Loan customers through MBNA Europe Bank Limited who are authorised and regulated by the Financial Services Authority.
