How do I apply?

Simple. Just click here to order an application form.  Or call us on 08456 10 20 20.

What are the charges

Our funds have no initial charges or bid offer spread, so whether you're buying or selling units the price is the same.

Our charges on the the Index tracking fund are an annual management fee of 1% a year and a 0.5% charge when we sell your units.

The Income Trust has a management fee of 1% a year, and that's it.

Can I invest in both trusts?

Yes.  Whether you're interested in income, going for growth, or after a mixture of the two, there are no limits on our unit trusts so you can invest as much as like into each fund.

What returns can I expect from a Virgin Unit Trust?

That depends partly on whether you invest in the Index Tracking Trust or the Income Trust, and partly on the performance of the investments.

If you're interested in the past performance of our unit trusts click here for full details.

How do I check how my savings are doing?

We'll send you a full statement twice a year to let you know how your savings are doing.  You can also phone or check out our website any time for the latest valuation.

How can I find out more?

You'll find more information online about our unit trust investment approaches on the ISA part of our website (Virgin's unit trusts are the engine room behind Virgin ISAs). There are graphs showing past performance of the stock market, comparisons between index tracking and active fund management, plus a full list of all the companies whose bonds we invest in.

Click here to go to our ISA site or click here to check out the unit prices of our unit trusts.

Don't forget, many people can save tax by using up their ISA allowance first, before investing direct into unit trusts.
