Virgin Life Cover - In a Nutshell

Here's a quick rundown of what Virgin Life Cover gives you:

  • A cash lump sum to help support your family and cover your debts if you die.
  • A choice of level term cover or reducing cover, depending on why you need it.
  • Built-in cover for terminal illness which pays out if you’re given less than a year to live.
  • A discount if you and your partner apply and pay for cover together.
  • Free life cover between exchange of contracts (or if you’re buying in Scotland, from the date the missives are concluded) and mortgage completion. Handy if you’re in the middle of buying a house.
  • And it starts from as little as 17p a day.              

For more detailed information, please check out the policy summary and full policy wordings.
