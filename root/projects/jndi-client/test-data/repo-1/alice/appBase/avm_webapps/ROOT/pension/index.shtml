Personal Pensions

Save for your retirement in the original low-cost, hassle-free pension

Virgin's award winning stakeholder pension is a doddle to set up. To get started click one of the options below:

I'm Employed

With the terrific tax breaks on offer, pensions are where the smart people are putting their money.

I'm Self Employed

Being able to stop, start and change your payments anytime without penalty makes our pension ideal for self-employed people.

I'm Not Employed

Start with as little as £1. Your relatives can even pay into your pension for you, boosting your savings further.
