We offer three types of pet insurance:

Accidental injury
The cheapest way of insuring your dog or cat, designed to help you cover one-off, unexpected costs if your pet is involved in an accident. It also includes lots of other benefits too. If you think pet insurance is a luxury you can't afford, this could help you think again.

Accident and illness
This covers your pet against accidents and illness, with a maximum time and amount we'll pay out for each condition - so premiums are kept affordable.

Accident and illness extra
Maximum cover at a competitive price. It covers your pet from the start of any accident or illness for the rest of its life or until you reach the maximum amount we'll pay out.
