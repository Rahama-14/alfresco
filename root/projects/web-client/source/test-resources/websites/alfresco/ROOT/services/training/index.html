Alfresco Training

We can provide a full range of training services for users, administrators and developers working with the Alfresco platform.

Training Courses

We currently provide the following training courses in association with our training partners.

  • Alfresco for Users and Administrators
  • Alfresco for Developers
  • Alfresco Combined Course

Alfresco for Users and Administrators provides a solid introduction to the Alfresco Enterprise Content Management System.

Alfresco for Developers builds on this training and covers everything that you need to know in order to work with the Alfresco ECM platform as a developer.

The Alfresco Combined Course is ideal for those wanting to learn about Alfresco as quickly as possible. This course combines the teaching from Alfresco for Users and Administrators and Alfresco for Developers into a single five-day programme.
