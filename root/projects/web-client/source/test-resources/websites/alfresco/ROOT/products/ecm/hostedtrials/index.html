Alfresco Hosted Trials

An Hosted Trial lets you try out Alfresco quickly and easily without having to install anything on your PC. With access via a web browser, you can get started straight away.

There are two simple steps in setting this up. First, we ask you to register with us by filling out some basic details about yourself. After you've done this, we'll take care of setting up your trial and we'll send you all the details you need to get started via email.

You can choose between two types of hosted trial, depending on your requirements. If you're not sure which one is right for you then please feel free to contact us at sales@alfresco.org.

Instant Trial

This is our basic trial that is intended to give you a quick and easy introduction to Alfresco over the web. You get:

  • Instant access using a shared username and password for seven days
  • Full access to a shared space where you can experiment with manging content

Click here to register for our Instant Trial.

Dedicated Company Trial

Our company trial is well-suited to groups of people or organisations who wish to evaluate Alfresco as an ECM tool. You get:

  • A dedicated account that we'll set up for you within 48 hours
  • Exclusive access to your own dedicated area for 30 days
  • The option to set up additional accounts for other members of your team, with access to a common shared area

Click here to register for our Company Trial.

Downloading Alfresco

Once you've tried Alfesco using our Hosted Trials, you might want to try out some of the more advanced functionality that Alfresco provides, such as user and group administration, WebDAV, CIFS and FTP integration or LDAP authentication. Our Alfresco Enterprise Trial allows you to download a trial version of Alfresco that you can install in your own infrastructure for free for up to thirty days.

Alternatively, you can download our open source version of Alfresco from our public download page.
