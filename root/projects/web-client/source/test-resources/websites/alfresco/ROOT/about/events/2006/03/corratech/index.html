CorraTech CEO and Alfresco CMO Present on Open Source Applications

Lille, France—February 28th, 2006—Alfresco Software Inc., the first provider of an open source enterprise content management solution, today announced its participation in the CorraTech Enterprise Open Source Applications Conference, Lille, Marcq-en Baroeul, Cite des Echanges, 3rd March 2006.

This conference will be led by Mr. Ron BONGO, CEO of Corra Technology, inc., Mr. Anthony BONNEVILLE, Managing Director of CorraTech France, Mrs. Corinne COPPIN, CorraTech’s European Business Development Manager and Dr. Ian Howells, Chief Marketing Officer of Alfresco.

The agenda is:

  • Introduction to Open Source Technologies
  • Open Source CRM : SugarCRM – Presentation and cases studies
    • Open Source Enterprise Content Management : Alfresco
  • The ECM solution to achieve increased usability, flexibility and extendibility at a lower TCO.
  • How to ensure durability and reliability of Open Source Applications

If interested, please send an email to questions@corratech.fr

NOTE: the conference will be run both in French and English with real time professional translation.

About Alfresco

Alfresco is the leading open source alternative for enterprise content management. It is the first company to bring the power of open source to the enterprise content management market, enabling unprecedented scale and a much lower total cost of ownership than proprietary systems. Founded by a team of content management veterans that includes the co-founder of Documentum, John Newton and former COO of Business Objects, John Powell. Alfresco is based in London.
