Alfresco Partner Network

Enterprise Content Management is growing at a forecasted compound annual growth rate of 19%, to be a $3.9bn market by 2008 (Source Forrester, 2005).  Recent surveys show 56% of North American firms using open source today with 19% planning on using it. Hand in hand with this trend, open source is moving up the stack from the operating system - Linux, to the RDBMS - MySQL, to the Application Server - JBoss and now to Enterprise Content Management – Alfresco. This presents a tremendous opportunity both for the open source software community and the partner ecosystem around it.

The Alfresco Partner Network Program is designed to provide partner organizations with the expertise to market, configure, customize and implement Alfresco solutions for customers worldwide. Alfresco works with different categories of partners including:

  • System Integrators
  • Infrastructure Providers
  • ISV/OEM Partners
  • Distributor/Resellers
  • Hosting Partners
  • Training

By signing up to the Alfresco Partner Network Program you will be making a statement to the market that you are able to provide a trusted, supported solution. The program offers several different levels of membership to align with your company's size and business model.
