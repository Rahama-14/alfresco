Alfresco Developer Assistance

Customers and Partners who are developing new Alfresco code benefit from the reassurance that Alfresco Developer Assistance can offer.

“If you are customizing or extending Alfresco, then getting Alfresco Developer Assistance ensures you can get the right advice on architecting your solution for best practice, best performance and easiest maintenance. You get access to the people that build Alfresco - there is not a better source of knowledge and experience."

The benefits include:

  • Faster time to market
  • Shorter development cycles
  • Fast track assistance
  • Developer peer network
  • Lower maintenance costs

Alfresco Developer Assistance gives you true a developer-to-developer network, allowing you to put your developers in direct contact with Alfresco's, giving you first-hand access to unparalleled levels of knowledge and experience.

When you request Alfresco Developer Assistance, rest assured that you’ll be assigned a developer with subject matter expertise for your specific issue. Alfresco developers can provide you with practical, insightful assistance on almost any Alfresco development topic. What’s more, they are always eager to help you incorporate the latest technology innovations to give your application a competitive edge.

Alfresco has a very experienced team of Developers who can help yours in all of the following areas:
  • Architectural Overview referencing both current Alfresco product and future plans
  • Alfresco Design Guidance and Specification overview
  • Recommendations on Best Practice
  • Advice on standard Methodology and configuration
  • Resolving specific API issues encountered during your Alfresco application development cycle

Alfresco Developer Assistance Features

With Alfresco Developer Assistance an annual fee opens the door for two of your team to pass your requests to the Alfresco Developers.

  • Alfresco Developer Assistance is available to Supported customers using the Alfresco Enterprise Edition
  • To request Alfresco Developer Assistance, please email the details of your request to support@alfresco.com. quoting your Alfresco Developer Assistance Code.
  • A request is defined as assistance with one issue, problem, or question relating to the use of a supported component of Alfresco regardless of the number of communications required. A single request is a problem that cannot be broken down into subordinate problems. If a problem consists of subordinate problems, each is considered a separate request.
  • Alfresco commit to respond to your request within two business days
  • Entry level Alfresco Developer Assistance allows you to pass up to 15 requests to the Developers who wrote the Alfresco code.
  • The advice and assistance offered does not extend to the composition or delivery of code. Our Partners consulting teams will be happy to help if this is what you need.
  • If you are not sure whether your request is one for the Alfresco Developers or not, then please email it to support@alfresco.com so that we can help you decide.


In addition you will get access to the Alfresco Developers and our latest technology through recorded and interactive update sessions. You will also receive a login to the Alfresco Developer Assistance community, moderated by Alfresco experts.
