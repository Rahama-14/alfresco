Platform Overview

The Alfresco Small Business Network is designed for small organizations that want to get the benefit of a simple-to-install, easy-to-use alternative to Microsoft® SharePoint®. This package can only be purchased for limited installations and configurations.

Please contact sales@alfresco.com for more information.

Our goal is to couple the innovation of the open source development and distribution model to surpass proprietary, commercial offerings in terms of features, functionality and benefits at a significantly lower price. Alfresco SMB is:

  • Stable and Reliable
  • Certified
  • Customer Support
  • Customer Knowledge Base Portal
  • Maintenance Updates
  • Bugs and Patch Support
  • Supported by Alfresco and its Certified Partners
