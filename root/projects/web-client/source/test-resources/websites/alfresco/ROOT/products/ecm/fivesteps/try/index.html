Trying Out Alfresco

We want as many people as possible to try out our software. We've therefore made it as easy as possible for anyone to try out both our open source and our commercial versions of Alfresco.

All our open source products are available in a number of different packages from our community site under a modified form of the Mozilla Public Licence. See our Legal and License page for more information.
