Platform Overview

The Alfresco Enterprise Network is the leading open source alternative for Enterprise Content Management. It couples the innovation of open source with the stability of a true enterprise-class platform built by a team of leading members from Documentum® and Interwoven® including the co-founder of Documentum. This is delivered at a small business price. It is designed for:

  • Corporations, Governments and other organizations looking for a production-ready open source ECM solution

Our goal is to not only to provide an open source offering but to surpass commercial offerings in terms of features, functionality and benefits to the user community. It is:

  • Stable and Reliable
  • Certified
  • Customer Support
  • Customer Knowledge Base Portal
  • Maintenance Updates
  • Bugs and Patch Support
  • Indemnity and Warranty
  • Supported by Alfresco and its Certified Partners
